Select CITYWALK is spread out over 6 lakh sq. ft. of retail space and is home to more than 175 brands. It offers the widest range of labels, we assure you – if there’s something you need, want or is a part of your wish list, your wishes will be fulfilled! Not only do we house a plethora of stores, but we also bring you a number of high-street brands to provide a lively outdoor shopping experience, numerous carts and kiosks to pick up fun, exciting products spread across the centre!

Select CITYWALK is home to coveted international beauty brands, absolutely-must-have clothing labels, a selection of restaurants serving delicious food and much more. In addition to being the city’s favourite destination to catch up with friends and a place to relax for the family, we are also a paradise for every shopaholic.
